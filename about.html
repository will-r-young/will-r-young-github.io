About Me:

A little bit about myself and this website:

I am student at Santa Barbara City College taking a variety of different courses.

My interests range from Digital Design to Astrophysics. I find that the balance between science and art is essential to my wellbeing and the line between them is far thinner than most people think.

This website is a means to display the work I have created while enrolled in the Digital Drawing class taught by Professor Gallegos.

I hope you enjoy!
